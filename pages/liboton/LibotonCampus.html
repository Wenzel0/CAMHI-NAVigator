CAMHI - Liboton

The Liboton Campus of Camarines Sur National High School (CSNHS), the oldest national high school in the province and one of the largest public secondary schools in the Bicol Region. Located along M.T. Villanueva Avenue in Naga City, this campus primarily hosts the school's Senior High School (SHS).
