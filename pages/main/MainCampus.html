CAMHI - Main

The Main Campus The Main CamHi school refers to Camarines Sur National High School (CSNHS), located on Peñafrancia Avenue, Zone 1, Brgy. Peñafrancia, Naga City. Established in 1902, it is the oldest national high school in the province and serves thousands of students in the Bicol Region.
